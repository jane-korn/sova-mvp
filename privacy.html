Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 December 2025

Jane Korneyko trading as Sova ("Sova", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address - when you use the assessment tool or contact us
  • Assessment responses - your answers to business health check questions
  • Contact information - when you reach out via email

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

We use browser storage (not traditional cookies) to enhance your experience:

  • localStorage - saves your assessment progress so you don't lose your work if you close the browser. Stored locally on your device only.
  • sessionStorage - remembers temporary preferences during your visit (e.g., dismissed notifications). Cleared when you close your browser.

1.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts - we use Google's font hosting service for typography. Google may collect usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising technologies. We do not sell your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the assessment tool functionality
  • Save your progress so you can return to incomplete assessments
  • Generate your personalised health check report
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

  • Local storage - Assessment data is stored in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to our servers.
  • Email communications - Stored securely and only accessed by authorised personnel.
  • Security measures - We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention

  • Browser storage - Remains until you clear your browser data or we update our storage mechanisms
  • Email communications - Retained for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law
  • Assessment data - You can clear your saved assessment at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage

5. Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Complaint - Lodge a complaint about how we handle your personal information
